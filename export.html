How Our Export Process Works

We manage every detail from product selection to on-site delivery.

01

Consultation & Specification

We work with your architects, engineers, or project managers to review plans, recommend the right products, and ensure compliance with local building codes and wind-load requirements for your region.

02

Product Sourcing & Manufacturing

We source from America's top impact-rated manufacturers â€” PGT, CGI, Custom Window Systems, ES Windows, and others. Custom sizes, finishes, and configurations are standard. Every unit is NOA-approved and hurricane-tested.

03

Quality Inspection & Warehousing

All products are received, inspected, and staged at our Miami-area warehouse. We verify every unit against your order specs â€” dimensions, finish, hardware, glass type â€” before anything gets packed.

04

Custom Crating & Container Loading

Windows and doors are custom-crated for ocean transit to prevent damage. We handle full container loads (FCL) and less-than-container loads (LCL), optimizing freight costs based on your project volume.

05

Shipping & Customs Coordination

We coordinate ocean freight from Port of Miami to your destination, including all export documentation, customs clearance support, and duty/tariff guidance. We work with trusted freight forwarders across every major Caribbean port.

06

On-Site Support & Installation

Need installation support? We can send our trained crews to your project site or provide detailed installation guides, technical support calls, and on-site supervision for your local contractors. We don't disappear after the container ships.
