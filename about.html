Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Quality

Since 1999, Trendex Windows & Doors has been South Florida's trusted partner for impact-rated windows, doors, and hurricane protection. What started as a commitment to protect families from devastating storms has grown into a full-service operation serving Miami-Dade, Broward, Palm Beach, and the Florida Keys.

We provide personalized customer service with fast turnaround — because we know that protecting your home or business shouldn't mean waiting months. Our team offers endless design options, the latest styles and trends, all at the best values in the market.

Every project gets our full attention. From initial consultation through final installation, our highly trained professionals work directly with architects, project managers, and contractors to ensure a flawless result.
